Account Deletion Request

To request the deletion of your Slip-Scan account and associated data, please send an email to our support team.

Click the button below to open your email client with the necessary details pre-filled, or manually compose an email with the following information:

  • To: support@appcraftstudios.com
  • Subject: Account Deletion Request
  • Body: Please state the email address or username associated with the Slip-Scan account you wish to delete.
Request Account Deletion via Email

Data Deletion Information

Upon successful processing of your account deletion request, the following data associated with your account will be permanently deleted:

  • User profile information (e.g., name, email)
  • Saved application settings and preferences
  • All scanned invoice data
  • Any generated reports

Please note: There is no data retention period. Once deleted, your data cannot be recovered.
